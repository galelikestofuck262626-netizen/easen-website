高端咖啡厅家具工程案例Custom Furniture for a Contemporary Café Space

从空间规划到家具落地的完整商业配置方案A complete commercial furniture solution from space planning to installation

本项目为一处高端商业咖啡厅空间，整体定位以轻松、自然的都市咖啡体验为核心。大面积落地玻璃、暖木色调、金属网格吊顶与户外绿景构成主要设计语言。我们的挑战：让家具成为建筑的一部分，而非仅仅摆放其中。 This project involved a high-end commercial café positioned around a calm, natural urban coffee experience. Floor-to-ceiling glass, warm wood tones, metal grid ceilings and outdoor greenery defined the space. Our challenge: furniture that felt part of the architecture — not simply placed inside it.

项目背景Project Background

空间以精品咖啡、轻餐与社交体验为定位，客户希望家具能与建筑气质统一，而非与之竞争——大面积玻璃幕墙、暖色灯光与户外景观是核心设计元素。 The space was designed as a contemporary café blending premium coffee, light dining and social interaction. The client wanted furniture that unified the space — not competed with its glass walls, warm lighting and outdoor views.

客户并非单件采购，而是委托我们提供从靠窗区、卡座区到休闲区和吧台的整体分区家具配置方案。 Rather than selecting individual pieces, the client approached us for a full-zone furniture configuration covering window seating, booth areas, lounge zones and bar counter positions.

客户核心需求Client Requirements

01

设计调性统一。家具需融入空间的自然、克制气质，不能出现厚重或商业感过强的产品。Design harmony. Furniture must match the café's natural, restrained aesthetic — no heavy commercial pieces disrupting the lightness of the space.

02

商业级耐用性。高频使用场景下，座椅需承受长期坐压、移动、清洁和搬运，结构稳定性和面料耐磨性至关重要。Commercial durability. High-traffic daily use means seating must withstand constant pressure, movement, cleaning and repositioning without degrading.

03

分区配置方案。靠窗区适合快速停留，卡座区适合长时间停留，休闲区需要视觉记忆点，吧台区需呼应咖啡设备。Zone-specific solutions. Window area for quick visits, booth area for extended stays, lounge zone for social seating and a bar counter configuration.

04

色彩协调统一。暖棕木饰面、黑色皮质坐垫、条纹布艺与银色金属脚，不抢空间但要有质感。Cohesive colour palette. Warm brown wood, black leather cushions, stripe fabric and silver metal legs — nothing overpowering the clean architectural lines.

我们的家具方案Our Furniture Solution

靠窗区Window Zone

细金属脚架木质靠背餐椅配黑色软包坐垫，视觉轻盈不遮挡落地窗景观，圆桌尺寸适合双人咖啡社交，兼顾翻台效率。Slim metal-leg dining chairs with wooden backs and black cushioned seats. Minimal frame keeps views unobstructed. Round tables optimised for two — for turnover and social coffee stops.

吧台区Bar Counter Zone

黑色皮面与暖棕木色结合的休闲椅，呼应深色柜体与咖啡设备，细金属结构与木质扶手形成对比，既有复古感也符合现代咖啡厅审美。Black leather and warm brown wood leisure chairs echoing the coffee equipment and dark cabinetry. Slim metal structure with wooden armrests — a vintage-modern contrast.

卡座与休闲区Booth & Lounge Zone

暖棕色皮质软包卡座适合阅读、办公和长时间停留，条纹布艺单椅作为视觉焦点，增强空间识别度与层次感。Warm brown upholstered booth seating for extended stays — reading, working, meeting. Stripe-fabric accent chairs as focal pieces creating spatial identity and depth.

生产质量控制Production Control

坐垫厚度、靠背角度、软包包边、金属脚稳定性均进行细化控制，确保视觉效果与商业使用性能双达标。Frame structure, cushion thickness, back angle, upholstery edge detail and metal leg stability were all controlled to ensure pieces matched both visual design and commercial performance.

落地效果Project Result

安装完成后，家具与咖啡厅整体空间高度融合。自然光透过落地窗柔化了木质座椅与暖棕软包的质感，暖色灯具进一步烘托出舒适氛围。家具没有抢占建筑与景观的主角位置，而是成为连接人与空间的媒介。 After installation, the furniture integrated naturally with the café's architectural language. Natural light softened the wood tones and warm upholstery. The furniture did not compete with the space — it completed it.
