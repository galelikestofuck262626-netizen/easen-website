Quality & Compliance

As a commercial restaurant and hotel furniture manufacturer, we understand that international buyers require more than attractive designs. Safety, durability, material compliance and stable production quality are essential.

Fire Safety Options

We can provide upholstery, foam and seating constructions tested to EN 1021-1/2, BS 5852, TB 117-2013 and NFPA 260.

Commercial-Grade Construction

Our booth seating and banquette furniture are built for high-traffic environments with reinforced frames, high-density foam and durable upholstery.

Low-Emission Material Options

We support low-VOC, low-formaldehyde solutions including E0/E1 grade panels, CARB/TSCA Title VI compliant plywood and low-emission adhesives.

Responsible Wood Sourcing

For European and international projects, FSC/PEFC certified wood materials and traceability documents can be arranged per project requirements.

Project Quality Control

Every project follows a step-by-step inspection: raw material checking, frame inspection, upholstery finishing, dimensional verification, packaging inspection and pre-shipment photos.

Certification Documents Available Upon Request

Certification and testing documents can be provided per project requirements for fire-retardant materials, low-emission panels, commercial-grade upholstery and responsible wood sourcing.
