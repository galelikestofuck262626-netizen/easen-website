专注商业家具，服务全球市场 Commercial Furniture Experts for Global Markets

EASEN Furniture 成立于2016年，总部位于中国家具生产基地——广东佛山乐从。拥有10000+平方米专业工厂，建立完善的生产体系，产品涵盖餐厅、酒店、咖啡厅等各类商业家具。 EASEN Furniture was founded in 2016 and is headquartered in Lecong, Foshan — China's furniture manufacturing hub. With over 10,000 m² of production space, we manufacture commercial furniture for restaurants, hotels, cafés and other hospitality spaces.

我们不只是卖家具，而是帮助客户把商业空间做对。从图纸审核、样品确认、批量生产到包装发货，每一个环节都有严格的质量控制。 We don't just sell furniture — we help clients get their commercial spaces right. From drawing review and sample confirmation to mass production and packaging, every step is quality-controlled.
