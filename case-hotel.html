项目背景Project Background

该项目位于美国纽约，是一个定位高端的城市酒店项目。酒店整体空间融合了现代奢华、东方艺术感与纽约都市气质，公共区域包含大堂休闲区、酒廊、会客区、泳池休闲区及部分套房空间。

从现场效果可以看到，酒店大量运用了深色木饰面、暖色灯光、金属细节、壁炉、水景、艺术装置以及大面积落地窗，整体氛围沉稳、私密、精致，强调高级酒店空间中的舒适体验与视觉层次。

我们非常荣幸参与了该项目中的一部分软包家具供应，包括部分沙发、休闲椅、单椅、卡座及配套餐椅等产品。虽然本次合作并不是整套酒店家具全案，但能够参与这样高规格的纽约酒店项目，对我们来说依然是一段非常有价值的国际项目经验。Located in New York, USA, this is a high-end urban hotel project. The hotel space blends modern luxury, Eastern artistic sensibility, and New York urban character, with public areas including lobby lounges, bars, reception areas, poolside leisure zones, and select suite spaces.

From the site, you can see the hotel extensively uses dark wood finishes, warm lighting, metal details, fireplaces, water features, art installations, and large floor-to-ceiling windows — creating a calm, private, and refined atmosphere that emphasizes comfort and visual layering in premium hotel spaces.

We are honored to have participated in supplying upholstered furniture for this project, including sofas, lounge chairs, armchairs, booth seating, and matching dining chairs. Although this collaboration wasn't a complete hotel furniture package, being part of such a high-caliber New York hotel project remains a valuable international experience for us.

客户背景与项目需求Client Background & Requirements

客户希望软包家具能够与酒店整体空间设计高度融合。由于项目本身属于高端酒店空间，家具不只是简单的功能产品，而是空间气质的一部分。The client wanted upholstered furniture to blend seamlessly with the hotel's overall spatial design. As a high-end hotel project, furniture is not merely functional but part of the spatial character.

客户对本次软包家具提出了几个非常明确的要求：The client outlined several clear requirements for the upholstered furniture:

01

家具风格必须与酒店整体设计统一。酒店空间以深木色、暖棕色、米灰色、黑色金属和局部橙红色点缀为主，整体氛围高级、安静、有层次。因此软包家具不能过于商业化，也不能过于厚重，而是要呈现出克制、舒适、耐看的高级感。Furniture style must align with the hotel's overall design. The hotel space features dark wood, warm brown, off-gray, black metal, and touches of orange-red — creating a premium, quiet, and layered atmosphere. Upholstered furniture should not be overly commercial or heavy, but rather exhibit restrained, comfortable, and enduring sophistication.

02

产品需要适合酒店高频使用。酒店大堂、酒廊、休闲区每天会接待大量客人，软包家具必须具备稳定的结构、良好的回弹性、耐磨面料以及便于后期维护的特性。客户不仅关注产品刚落地时的效果，也关注长期运营后的状态。Products must withstand high-frequency hotel use. Hotel lobbies, bars, and lounges receive numerous guests daily. Upholstered furniture must have stable structure, good resilience, wear-resistant fabrics, and easy maintenance. The client cares about both initial appearance and long-term operational condition.

03

坐感要舒适，但造型不能松垮。高端酒店的休闲区家具需要让客人愿意停留，但同时也必须保持清晰的轮廓和挺括的外观。座面、靠背、扶手的饱满度和支撑感都需要经过平衡，不能过软，也不能显得生硬。Seating must be comfortable without appearing sloppy. High-end hotel lounge furniture should invite guests to linger while maintaining clean silhouettes and crisp appearance. Seat, backrest, and armrest fullness and support must be balanced — neither too soft nor too rigid.

04

细节工艺要经得起近距离观察。酒店空间灯光层次丰富，家具经常处在客人近距离接触的位置，因此软包的车线、转角、收口、弧度、靠背高度、坐垫厚度等细节都会被放大。客户要求产品整体精致，不能有明显褶皱、变形、线条歪斜或比例不协调的问题。Detail craftsmanship must withstand close inspection. Hotel spaces have rich lighting layers, and furniture is often positioned where guests can closely examine it. Stitching, corners, finishes, curves, backrest height, and cushion thickness will all be magnified. The client requires overall refinement without visible wrinkles, deformation, misaligned lines, or disproportionate elements.

软包家具解决方案Upholstered Furniture Solution

根据酒店不同区域的功能和氛围，我们为项目提供了多种类型的软包家具配合。Based on the function and atmosphere of different hotel areas, we provided various types of upholstered furniture to complement each space.

酒廊和会客区域Bar & Reception Areas

家具以低重心沙发、休闲椅和组合座椅为主，整体造型简洁而饱满，搭配暖灰、米色、深棕和橙红色软装，使空间在沉稳中保留一定的活力。Furniture centers on low-profile sofas, lounge chairs, and modular seating. Clean yet voluminous forms paired with warm gray, beige, deep brown, and orange-red upholstery bring vitality to the沉稳 atmosphere.

大堂休闲区Lobby Lounge Area

软包家具更强调开放式会客体验。单椅、双人沙发、圆弧造型座椅与茶几组合在一起，形成多个独立但不割裂的交流区域，既适合短暂停留，也适合商务会面。Upholstered furniture emphasizes open reception experiences. Armchairs, loveseats, curved seating, and coffee tables combine to form multiple distinct yet connected conversation zones — suitable for brief stays or business meetings.

泳池及休闲空间Poolside & Leisure Spaces

家具需要与高挑空间、水景、灯光和木饰面形成呼应。我们在软包比例和面料选择上更注重轻盈感和舒适度，使家具不会压迫空间，同时保证足够的使用稳定性。Furniture needs to echo the high ceilings, water features, lighting, and wood finishes. We focused on lighter proportions and fabric choices for a sense of airiness and comfort — ensuring furniture doesn't overwhelm the space while maintaining stability.

套房和私密休息区域Suites & Private Rest Areas

家具则更强调安静、舒适和居住感。沙发和休闲椅采用更柔和的面料肌理，搭配暖色灯光和深色木质背景，营造出高级酒店套房中放松、私密的氛围。Furniture emphasizes tranquility, comfort, and residential feel. Sofas and lounge chairs use softer fabric textures paired with warm lighting and dark wood backgrounds to create a relaxing, private atmosphere in premium hotel suites.

项目执行过程Project Execution

本次项目虽然只涉及部分软包家具，但我们依然按照酒店工程项目的标准进行对接和生产。Although this project only involved partial upholstered furniture, we followed hotel project standards for coordination and production.

前期确认Pre-Production

我们根据客户提供的空间效果图、现场照片和产品需求，对每一类软包家具的尺寸比例、面料颜色、坐感要求和使用区域进行了确认。由于酒店不同区域的空间尺度差异较大，我们特别关注家具与现场空间的比例关系，避免产品落地后出现过大、过小或视觉不协调的问题。Based on spatial renderings, site photos, and product requirements provided by the client, we confirmed dimensions, fabric colors, seating feel, and usage zones for each type of upholstered furniture. Given the varying spatial scales across hotel areas, we paid special attention to furniture-to-space proportions to avoid sizing or visual coordination issues after installation.

材料选择Material Selection

在材料选择方面，我们重点考虑了酒店商业使用环境。软包面料需要具备较好的耐磨性、舒适触感和视觉质感，同时也要便于日常清洁维护。填充部分选用高密度回弹海绵，使座面在长期使用中保持支撑性和回弹性。For material selection, we prioritized the hotel's commercial use environment. Upholstery fabrics needed good wear resistance, comfortable touch, and visual quality while being easy to clean and maintain. High-density resilient foam was selected for cushioning to maintain support and bounce over long-term use.

结构制作Structural Construction

在结构制作方面，我们加强了座位受力区域、靠背连接位置和扶手边角位置的稳定性，确保产品能够适应酒店公共区域的高频使用。对于休闲椅、沙发和卡座类产品，我们特别控制了靠背角度、座深和座高，使产品既符合人体舒适度，也符合酒店空间的视觉比例。For structural construction, we reinforced stress-bearing areas of seats, backrest connections, and armrest corners to ensure products could withstand high-frequency use in hotel public areas. For lounge chairs, sofas, and booths, we carefully controlled backrest angles, seat depth, and seat height for both ergonomic comfort and visual proportion within hotel spaces.

软包工艺Upholstery Craftsmanship

在软包工艺方面，我们重点把控了面料张力、边角弧度、车线顺直度、坐垫饱满度和靠背成型效果。尤其是圆弧椅、低靠背沙发和异形软包产品，对工艺要求更高，需要保证造型自然、线条流畅、左右对称。For upholstery craftsmanship, we focused on fabric tension, corner curves, stitching straightness, cushion fullness, and backrest shaping. Curved chairs, low-back sofas, and irregular upholstered pieces required even higher craftsmanship to ensure natural forms, smooth lines, and left-right symmetry.

出货与包装Shipping & Packaging

出货前，我们对产品进行了外观检查、尺寸检查、坐感检查和包装保护。由于项目位于纽约，长途运输对包装要求较高，因此我们在包装环节对边角、软包面、脚部和易受压位置进行了加固保护，尽量降低运输过程中的磕碰和压痕风险。Before shipping, we conducted appearance checks, dimension verification, seating feel tests, and packaging protection. Given the New York location and long-distance shipping requirements, we reinforced protection for corners, upholstery surfaces, legs, and pressure-prone areas to minimize damage risks during transit.

落地效果Project Results

项目落地后，软包家具与酒店整体空间融合度较高。家具没有抢夺建筑和室内设计本身的视觉焦点，而是以舒适、安静、细腻的方式提升了空间体验。After installation, the upholstered furniture integrated well with the hotel's overall space. The furniture didn't compete with architectural and interior design focal points but enhanced the spatial experience through comfort, tranquility, and refinement.

●

酒廊区域，沙发和休闲椅与深色吧台、暖色灯带、壁炉和木饰面形成统一氛围，使空间呈现出纽约高端酒店特有的沉稳感和私密感。Bar area: Sofas and lounge chairs unified with dark bars, warm lighting strips, fireplaces, and wood finishes — creating the calm and private atmosphere characteristic of New York luxury hotels.

●

公共休闲区，米灰色、暖棕色和局部橙红色软包家具让空间更有层次，既保持了高级感，也避免了大面积深色空间带来的压抑感。Public lounge area: Off-gray, warm brown, and touches of orange-red upholstered furniture added layering to the space, maintaining sophistication while avoiding the oppressive feel of large dark areas.

●

泳池与休息区域，浅色软包座椅与水面、灯光、立柱和大型吊灯形成呼应，让空间更具度假感和放松感，同时也保留了高端酒店应有的秩序感。Poolside and rest areas: Light upholstered seating echoed water surfaces, lighting, columns, and large chandeliers — creating a resort-like, relaxing feel while maintaining the order expected of premium hotels.

从整体效果来看，本次软包家具达到了客户对视觉品质、舒适体验和商业耐用性的要求。产品落地后，在空间中呈现自然，不突兀，不廉价，符合高端酒店公共区域对家具品质的基本标准。Overall, the upholstered furniture met client requirements for visual quality, comfort, and commercial durability. After installation, products appeared natural in the space — not jarring, not cheap — meeting the quality standards expected for high-end hotel public areas.

项目总结Project Summary

这次纽约酒店项目对我们来说具有重要意义。虽然我们只参与了其中一部分软包家具，但项目本身的标准很高，对设计理解、材料选择、软包工艺、生产细节和出口交付都提出了更高要求。

通过这个项目，我们更加深刻地认识到，高端酒店家具并不是单纯追求造型复杂或材料昂贵，而是要在空间风格、人体舒适度、商业耐用性和细节质感之间取得平衡。

我们也非常感谢客户给予我们参与该项目的机会。未来，我们将继续专注于酒店、餐厅、咖啡厅、酒吧等商业空间软包家具，为更多国际商业项目提供稳定、专业、有质感的家具产品与定制服务。This New York hotel project holds significant meaning for us. Although we only participated in part of the upholstered furniture, the project itself had high standards, demanding greater requirements for design understanding, material selection, upholstery craftsmanship, production details, and export delivery.

Through this project, we gained deeper insight that high-end hotel furniture isn't about pursuing complex forms or expensive materials, but rather achieving balance between spatial style, ergonomic comfort, commercial durability, and detail quality.

We are deeply grateful to the client for the opportunity to participate in this project. Going forward, we will continue focusing on upholstered furniture for hotels, restaurants, cafes, bars, and other commercial spaces — providing stable, professional, and quality furniture products and customization services for more international commercial projects.
